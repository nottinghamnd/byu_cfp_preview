Perfection for Nine Weeks

Despite being picked second to last in the Big 12 preseason rankings, BYU had one of the best starts to the season in school history, going 9-0. Not only was BYU winless, but wins over ranked SMU and Kansas State propelled the Cougars to top 10 in the nation and top of the Big 12.

Disaster

Confident in a playoff appearance and a Big 12 title, BYU went into the Kansas game unprepared, only to see the red-hot Jayhawks winning late in the fourth in Provo. Coming up short in crunch time, the Cougars, having been dealt their first and only loss, still had hope for the postseason.

Hope

The loss to Kansas was heartbreaking, but confidence was still high at BYU. They were one game short of clinching a Big 12 title spot, but needed a win in Tempe, Arizona.

Crushed

Cougars fight hard in Tempe, but once again coming up only three yards short on the hail mary attempt to win the game. Hopes for the postseason and conference title are crushed as Arizona State goes on to win the Big 12 and gets a playoff bid.
